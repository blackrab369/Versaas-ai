## Market Analysis
- **Target Market**: Local farmers and health-conscious consumers
- **Problem**: Farmers struggle with direct-to-consumer sales channels
- **Solution**: Mobile marketplace connecting farmers directly with local buyers
- **Market Size**: $2.8B local food market (growing 15% annually)

## Technical Requirements
- **Platform**: iOS and Android (cross-platform)
- **Features**: 
  - Farmer profiles and product listings
  - Consumer browsing and ordering
  - Payment processing
  - Delivery coordination
  - Inventory management
- **Tech Stack**: React Native, Node.js, PostgreSQL

## Revenue Model
- **Commission**: 8-12% on each transaction
- **Subscription**: Premium features for farmers ($29/month)
- **Advertising**: Featured listings and promotions
- **Target**: $1M revenue by day 180

## Competitive Analysis
- **Direct Competitors**: LocalHarvest, Farmigo
- **Indirect Competitors**: Instacart, Amazon Fresh
- **Differentiation**: Focus on local farms, direct relationships, transparency
